Education

I graduated from high school in 2022. In high school, I focused on working on my grades while also participating in the high school string orchestra and the robotics team, where I was a programmer.

I began studying as a computer science major at BYU in the fall of 2022. That year, I focused on accomplishing some GEs such as Statistics and American Heritage, while beginning on the entry-level CS courses, such as CS 111 and CS 235 (Data Structures). My goal with CS was to gain a broad understanding and then focus on the cybersecurity field. After a 2 year deferment to serve a mission, I decided to switch my major to cybersecurity to more fully pursue my professional interests, and so in 2025, I enrolled in an entry level cybersecurity class while continuing to take some CS classes.
